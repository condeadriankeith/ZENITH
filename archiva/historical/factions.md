# Faction Motivations 
 
 ## THE MAGISTRATES 
 * **Goal:** **CONTAINMENT** 
 * **Motivation:** To hide the Shards and enforce the Law of Silence. They believe that by keeping the Shards dormant and hidden, they can prevent the Great Filter from triggering. 
 
 ## THE VIREK ENGINEERS 
 * **Goal:** **EXTRACTION** 
 * **Motivation:** To find and use the Shards as power sources for their "God-Engines." They see the Shards as the ultimate fuel for industrial and technological advancement. 
 
 ## THE WHITE HAND 
 * **Goal:** **EVOLUTION** 
 * **Motivation:** To become the Shards through "Ascension Rites." They believe that humanity must mutate and harden to survive the coming Celestial Reset, effectively becoming cosmic entities themselves.
