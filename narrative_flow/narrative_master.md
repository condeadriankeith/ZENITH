# Narrative Master: ZENITH 
## Title: ZENITH: The Age of Inheritance 
**Logline:** To save the world below, he must burn the world above. 
**Core Theme:** The cost of defying the inevitable (The "Long Descent"). 
**Protagonist:** Maer (The Vault-Keeper). 
**Antagonist:** The Great Filter / Val-Kora (The Celestial). 
**Total Chapters:** 24 (Approx. 120,000 words). 
 
 --- 
 
 ## PART I: THE SILENCE OF THE ARCHIVES (Chapters 1–6) 
 *Focus: The "Normal" World, The Discovery, and The Fall from Grace.* 
 *Setting: Cyris (The Golden Sector) & The Rust Sprawl (Virek).* 
 
 * **Ch 1: The Dust of Aethelgard** (Written) - Maer finds the Codex. 
 * **Ch 2: The Library of Silence** (Written) - Maer escapes Cyris. 
 * **Ch 3: The Rust Sprawl** (Written) - Maer survives Virek. 
 * **Ch 4: The Engineer’s Bargain** - Meeting Eldra Voss. 
 * **Ch 5: The Blue Pulse** - Acquiring the Azure Shard. 
 * **Ch 6: The First Seal** - The Magistrates attack. 
 
 ## PART II: THE WEIGHT OF THE WILD (Chapters 7–12) 
 *Setting: The Deep Weald (Eirn & Sahru).* 
 * **Ch 7-12:** The hunt for the Veridian Shard (Life) and the battle with the Beast of Root. 
 
 ## PART III: THE CULT OF BLOOD (Chapters 13–18) 
 *Setting: The Ashlands (Thalen & Alkyr).* 
 * **Ch 13-18:** Infiltrating the White Hand to steal the Crimson Shard (Body). 
 
 ## PART IV: THE ASCENSION (Chapters 19–24) 
 *Setting: The Hollow (Nyth) & The Zenith.* 
 * **Ch 19-24:** Entering the Void, claiming the Violet Shard, and the final climb to the Zenith. 
 
 --- 
 
 ## Mechanics Index 
 * **The 5 Shards:** Gold (Law), Azure (Mind), Veridian (Life), Crimson (Body), Violet (Void). 
 * **The Bleed:** Cognitive erosion caused by proximity to unshielded Shards. 
 * **The Vault:** A lead/runed cylinder built by Eldra to contain the Shards.
