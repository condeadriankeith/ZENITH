# Scene Prompts

## Prologue Scene Prompt

### Core Direction
Write a polished cinematic prologue draft featuring the Traveler's encounter with the Book in the Hollow. The scene should be atmospheric, sensory, and reveal character through action rather than exposition.

### Required Elements
- **Setting:** The Hollow, a ruined sanctuary with an altar at its center
- **Character:** The Traveler (nameless, left-handed, carrying a pack of knowledge)
- **Action:** Touching the Book and retrieving a fragment of a pattern
- **Atmosphere:** Wounded, desolate space with prismatic light and sensory details
- **Dialogue:** The stones speak the canonical refrain: "Mortals should not meddle with the power of the Gods"

### Style Requirements
- Cinematic, sensory prose that avoids purple prose
- Left-hand motif present throughout
- Prismatic/oil-on-water imagery for magical manifestations
- Lean, efficient description that serves the scene's purpose
- The scene should feel like a movie sequence that readers can visualize

### Character Constraints
- The Traveler must be nameless throughout
- Consistently use left hand for bindings and inversions
- Reference the pack of knowledge and its contents
- Show the scar on the left wrist from previous bargains
- Include the emotional weight of the mother's traded name

### World Building Elements
- The Meridian Lock system should be demonstrated
- Magic should manifest with prismatic imagery
- The entropy consequences should be subtly shown
- The canonical refrain must appear verbatim
- The scene should establish the dangerous nature of the Traveler's actions

## Chapter 1 Scene Prompt

### Core Direction
Begin with the Traveler's memory of trading his mother's name ('Maer') to save a dying valley. This flashback should establish his emotional anchor and the cost of his nameless state.

### Required Elements
- **Setting:** A dying valley (flashback scene)
- **Character:** The Traveler, younger, with his mother's name
- **Action:** The ritual or bargain that trades the name
- **Atmosphere:** Poignant, regretful, but necessary
- **Connection:** Links to the present-day quest

### Style Requirements
- Balance intimate emotion with cosmic consequence
- Show the lullaby that has lost its last verse
- Use sensory details to ground the emotional moment
- Maintain cinematic quality while exploring internal state

## Chapter 2 Scene Prompt

### Core Direction
The Traveler arrives in Cyris, the Marble Dominion, observing the Codex Courts' operation and witnessing a dispute resolution that demonstrates the value of legal contracts as magical reality.

### Required Elements
- **Setting:** Cyris main plaza and court chamber
- **Character:** The Traveler observing; local officials
- **Action:** Witnessing the Codex Court process
- **Atmosphere:** Formal, marble-heavy, legally-focused
- **Magic System:** Covenant/Contract magic demonstration

### World Building Requirements
- Show the cultural importance of oaths and contracts
- Demonstrate how legal bindings are etched into the Meridian
- Include marble light and white fissure line imagery
- Contrast with the Traveler's nameless state

## Chapter 3 Scene Prompt

### Core Direction
In Thalen's singing groves, the Traveler participates in a naming ceremony that makes him acutely aware of his own missing name while learning about the value of names in the world.

### Required Elements
- **Setting:** Thalen forest and grove center
- **Character:** The Traveler; Grove Speakers or Shapers
- **Action:** Participating in naming ceremony
- **Atmosphere:** Living, breathing forest with names as life force
- **Magic System:** Spirit magic demonstration

### Character Development
- Show the Traveler's reaction to the groves' response to his nameless state
- Demonstrate the value of names through the grove's reaction to others
- Connect to his emotional anchor about the traded name

## Chapter 4 Scene Prompt

### Core Direction
Visit to Virek's Arc-Foundry ruins where the Traveler studies the Mercury-Keel prototype and learns more about the Fracture, exploring the tension between innovation and safety.

### Required Elements
- **Setting:** Virek foundry district and laboratory
- **Character:** The Traveler; possibly local engineers or historians
- **Action:** Examining the Mercury-Keel prototype
- **Atmosphere:** Industrial, brass-heavy, innovation-focused
- **Magic System:** Engineered/Techomancy demonstration

### Thematic Integration
- Explore the theme of balance vs. progress
- Connect to the larger consequences of the Fracture
- Show the Traveler's composite magic abilities
- Demonstrate the prismatic/brass spark imagery

## Chapter 5 Scene Prompt

### Core Direction
Flashback scene revealing the circumstances of Mentor Keth's disappearance and the Traveler's discovery of the crucial note about the Hollow: 'Hollow Seal: pair a star-shard with three ledger bones.'

### Required Elements
- **Setting:** Keth's study (flashback)
- **Character:** The Traveler; Mentor Keth; possible antagonists
- **Action:** Discovery of the crucial note
- **Atmosphere:** Tension, mystery, loss
- **Connection:** Sets the main quest in motion

### Emotional Development
- Show the relationship between Mentor and Student
- Demonstrate the promise that drives the Traveler
- Include the emotional weight of the mentor's fate

## General Scene Writing Guidelines

### Universal Requirements
- Each scene must advance plot, character, or theme
- Maintain consistency with established world building
- Include appropriate magic system manifestations
- Use sensory details to create immersion
- Follow canonical constraints and character traits

### Quality Standards
- Cinematic and visual in quality
- Lean prose without excessive description
- Meaningful dialogue that serves story goals
- Clear scene objectives and outcomes
- Strong opening and closing hooks

### Technical Constraints
- No exposition paragraphs exceeding 400 words
- Canonical phrases preserved verbatim where required
- Character traits maintained consistently
- Magic system rules followed without contradiction
- Left-hand motif and prismatic imagery integrated appropriately
