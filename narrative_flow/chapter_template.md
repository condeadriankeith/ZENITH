# Chapter Template

## Chapter Header Information
```
# Chapter [Number]: [Title]
**Estimated Word Count:** [Target word count]
**POV:** [Primary point of view character]
**Location:** [Primary location]
**Timeline:** [When this occurs in the story]
**Previous Chapter:** [What led to this chapter]
**Next Chapter:** [What this leads to]
```

## Opening Section
- **Hook:** Compelling opening that connects to the previous chapter and draws the reader in
- **Setting Grounding:** Establish the time, place, and immediate situation
- **Character State:** Show the POV character's emotional and physical condition
- **Stakes Reminder:** Reinforce what's at risk for the character and world

## Body Sections
### Section 1: Setup and Development
- **Scene A:** [Brief description of first scene]
  - Goal: What the character(s) are trying to accomplish
  - Obstacle: What prevents easy success
  - Conflict: Tension between characters, self, or environment
  - Revelation: New information or discovery

### Section 2: Escalation and Challenge
- **Scene B:** [Brief description of second scene]
  - Development: How the situation becomes more complex
  - Stakes: What gets raised or what's newly at risk
  - Challenge: New obstacle or increased difficulty
  - Choice: Decision that character must make

### Section 3: Crisis and Turning Point
- **Scene C:** [Brief description of third scene]
  - Crisis: Moment when everything seems to go wrong
  - Turning Point: How the chapter changes the overall story direction
  - Consequence: What results from the crisis
  - Bridge: Connection to the next chapter

## Closing Section
- **Resolution:** How the immediate chapter situation is addressed
- **Forward Momentum:** Set up for the next chapter
- **Thematic Resonance:** Reinforce the book's major themes
- **Cliffhanger/Transition:** End that compels reading the next chapter
