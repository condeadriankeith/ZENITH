## Regional Wisdom

### Cyris Sayings
> *"When the Locks wake and the Nodes seal, remember the price that mortals feel. The Veil remembers what we forgot, and the Refrain keeps us unbought."* — Children's Rhyme from Cyris

### Thalen Verses
> *"Names grow like rings in living bark, but cut too deep and silence marks. The memory fades, the voice turns thin, where no true name remembers kin."* — Verse from Thalen Groves

### Virek Admonitions
> *"Schematics drawn in brass and steel may crack the foundations we rely. The Keel that cuts through temporal field may sunder what we cannot tie."* — Engineer's Admonition from Virek

### Kallum Philosophy
> *"Salt preserves what water takes, but ledgers hold what memory breaks. A name once spent can't be refunded, by any tide that's ever bounded."* — Tide-Ledger Saying from Kallum

### Eirn Wisdom
> *"Ice preserves what stone forgets, and oaths endure what fire lets. Carved in frost, the promise stays, until the breaker pays the ways."* — Oathkeeper's Maxim from Eirn

### Alkyr Prayers
> *"Stars above, echoes below, between the two, our true names go. Listen well to Meridian winds, where Veil and Face both meet and blend."* — Alkyr Pilgrim's Prayer

### Mirel Chants
> *"In mists we hide what echoes know, the names we've lost and names we've sown. Veil whispers secrets to the shore, where memory and mist both pour."* — Mirel Mist-Seller's Chant

### Nyth Warnings
> *"In spaces thin where logic bends, the self may lose what being sends. Question not what "I" may mean, in places where no names are seen."* — Nyth Researcher's Warning

### Valen Wisdom
> *"Crossroads meet where paths align, but nodes that break affect all signs. Safe passage comes to those who pay the toll of keeping common way."* — Valen Trader's Wisdom
