## Usage Notes
- **Regional Dialects:** Use sensory-specific metaphors (e.g., "by the marble" in Cyris, "tide-willing" in Kallum).
- **The Left-Hand Motif:** Characters using left-hand rites should be treated with suspicion or awe.
- **Costs of Magic:** Always show the price a character pays for their abilities (memory loss, physical strain, or social debt).
