# Character Profile: Eldra Voss 
**Role:** The Engineer / Architect of the Vault 
**Location:** Engine 42, The Rust Sprawl (Virek) 
**Archetype:** The Mad Mechanic 

## Physical Description 
* **Appearance:** Small, wiry, stained with coal-dust. Wild red hair. 
* **Eyes:** Bright orange "Furnace-Gaze" (mutation from staring into engines). 
* **Gear:** Wears a harness of brass pressure gauges. Carries a massive, rune-etched iron wrench. 

## Personality 
* **Voice:** Fast, clipped, rhythmic. Interrupts often. Hates silence. 
* **Motivation:** Pressure Control. She wants to keep the city from exploding. 
* **Dynamic:** She thinks Maer is soft until he proves his technical skill.
