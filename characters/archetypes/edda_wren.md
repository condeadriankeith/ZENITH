A swift and resourceful courier who navigates the shifting silt-roads of the Valen plains, Edda carries messages that are too dangerous for the Meridian-linked postal networks. She is a master of "Silt-Skating," using specialized boots to glide over the unstable ground.

Edda is small and agile, her skin dusted with the fine, golden silt of the plains. She wears a tight-fitting leather suit and a series of light, aerodynamic scarves that flutter behind her as she moves. Her eyes are covered by large, amber-tinted goggles, and her boots are fitted with wide, flat "Silt-Blades" made of polished bone. She carries a waterproof satchel filled with sealed scrolls and message-cylinders. She smells of dry grass, sun-warmed leather, and the faint, sweet scent of Valen wildflowers.

Edda serves as a messenger and a source of local news. She provides the Traveler with the "Silt-Skates" needed to cross the unstable plains and helps them deliver vital messages to distant allies.

The soft, *shush-shush* of her silt-blades across the sand and the bright, bird-like whistle she uses to signal her arrival.

## Relationship to World Shards
- **Motivation:** Edda carries messages through the "Name-Bleed." She believes the Shards are "Cosmic Mail" that was never delivered.
- **Goal:** Deliver a "Warning-Sigil" from the High Peaks to the resistance in the Sprawl.

## Moral Contradictions
- She is a messenger who has forgotten who sent her.
- She values speed, but she is carrying a weight that slows her down to a crawl.

### Supplemental Lore: The Silt-Skating of Valen

In the central plains of Valen, the soil has been ground into a fine, energetic powder known as "Silt." This silt is highly sensitive to Meridian fluctuations, often behaving more like a liquid than a solid. Traditional travel is nearly impossible, as wagons and animals quickly sink into the "Silt-Pits."

The Messenger's Guild has developed the art of "Silt-Skating." Using boots fitted with "Silt-Blades"—long, flat pieces of polished bone or treated brass—messengers can glide over the surface of the silt, using the region's constant winds for propulsion. This requires a high degree of balance and a deep understanding of the "Silt-Currents" that flow through the plains. A skilled skater can travel hundreds of miles in a single day, but a single mistake can result in being swallowed by the thirsty ground.

### The Silt-Blades of Edda

Edda’s blades are carved from the ribs of a Great Silt-Walker, a creature that lived in the plains before the Fracture.

1. **The Bone-Edge:** Polished to a mirror finish to minimize friction with the silt.
2. **The Tension-Straps:** Made of treated leather and copper wire, allowing the blades to be adjusted for different silt-densities.
