# Character Profile: High Priest Kaelen 
**Role:** Leader of the White Hand 
**Location:** The Ashlands 
**Artifact:** The Crimson Shard (Body). 
**Archetype:** The Body Horror Messiah 

## Physical Description 
* **Mutation:** He has no skin; he is covered in white bone-plating. He has six fingers on each hand. 
* **Philosophy:** "Pain is evolution." He wants to mutate humanity to survive the end of the world. 

## Abilities 
* **Biomancy:** Can reshape flesh and bone with a touch.
