# Character Profile: High Inquisitor Oren Valis 
**Role:** The Ruler of Cyris / Warden of Law 
**Artifact:** The Gold Shard (Law) embedded in his throat. 
**Archetype:** The Perfect Tyrant 

## Abilities 
* **The Word:** He can speak Edicts that reality must obey (e.g., "Halt," "Silence"). 
* **The Network:** He is mentally linked to every White Skiff (drone) in the city. 

## Personality 
* **Motivation:** Stasis. He wants to freeze the world in perfect order to prevent the "Great Filter." 
* **Voice:** Melodic, terrifyingly calm. Never raises his voice.
