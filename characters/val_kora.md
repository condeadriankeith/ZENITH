# Character Profile: Val-Kora (The Great Filter) 
**Role:** The Celestial / Final Antagonist 
**Location:** The Zenith (The Star-Altar) 
**Artifact:** Seeks the Violet Shard (Void). 
**Archetype:** Cosmic Horror / Administrator 

## Nature 
* **Identity:** A "Celestial Administrator" sent to prune chaotic realities. 
* **Appearance:** A silhouette of violet light wearing a "Mask of Stars." 
* **The Threat:** Whenever Shards converge, Val-Kora wakes up to reset the world (The Great Pour). 
* **Presence:** Currently sleeping. Felt as the static of "The Bleed."
