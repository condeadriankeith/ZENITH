# Character Profile: Maer 
**Role:** The Keeper / The Vault-Bearer 
**Status:** Fugitive (Ex-Archivist) 
**Archetype:** The Reluctant Guardian 
**Weapon:** The Codex of the First Breath / A simple bone-handled knife / A silver stylus. 

## The Condition: "The Bleed" 
* **Definition:** Arcane Radiation Poisoning from carrying the Codex. 
* **Symptoms:** Loss of specific nouns and memories. Frequent silver nosebleeds. 
* **The Cost:** He has already sacrificed the memory of his *Childhood Home* to unlock the map. 
* **Motivation:** Atonement. He spent 20 years erasing history; now he is dying to save it. 

## Personality 
* **Voice:** Quiet, precise, observational. Speaks in complete sentences. 
* **Dynamic:** He acts as the "Logic" to Eldra's "Chaos."
