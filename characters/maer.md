# Character Profile: Maer 
**Role:** The Keeper / The Vault-Bearer 
**Status:** Fugitive (Ex-Archivist) 
**Weapon:** The Codex of the First Breath / A simple bone-handled knife. 
 
## The Condition: "The Bleed" 
* **Definition:** Arcane Radiation Poisoning. 
* **Symptoms:** Loss of specific nouns/memories. Silver nosebleeds. 
* **The Scar:** He has silver scars on his knuckles (from his time as a scribe). 
* **The Cost:** He has already lost the memory of his *Childhood Home* and his *Mother's Face* to unlock the Codex. 
 
## Personality 
* **Voice:** Quiet, precise, observational. He speaks in complete sentences. He is the opposite of Eldra. 
* **Motivation:** Atonement. He spent 20 years erasing history; now he wants to save it. 
* **Assets:** 
     * **The Codex:** A sentient book that acts as a map and a radiation source. 
     * **The Stylus:** A vibration-tool he uses as a bluff/weapon.
