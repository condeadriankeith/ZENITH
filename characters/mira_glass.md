# Character Profile: Senior Archivist Mira Glass 
**Role:** The Inquisitor 
**Allegiance:** The Magistrates (Cyris) 
**Archetype:** The True Believer 

## Gear & Abilities 
* **The Cane:** A glass walking stick filled with gold mercury. It detects resonance. 
* **The Lenses:** A brass rig on her arm that spins to scan thermal/magical signatures. 
* **Philosophy:** "Silence is Safety." She believes deleting history saves lives. 

## Personality 
* **Voice:** Cold, clinical, sharp. She speaks like she is reading a verdict.
