A blind hermit living on the highest peaks of Alkyr, Kael uses "Aspect-Witnessing" to perceive the world's Meridian Veil from a perspective that transcends physical sight. He is a philosopher of the sky, his mind as vast and cold as the thin air of the Great Stair.

Kael is an elderly man with skin that looks like weathered granite, etched with deep lines and star-like freckles. He is blind, his eyes replaced by two polished spheres of obsidian that reflect the starry sky even during the day. He wears simple, heavy wool robes and carries a staff made of "Aether-Wood" that hums with a faint, celestial frequency. He smells of thin air, mountain herbs, and the sharp, clean scent of snow.

Kael serves as a cosmic mentor, helping the Traveler understand the larger structure of the world and the nature of the "Architects." He provides the "Aether-Lens" needed to see the hidden node-paths in the sky.

The low, harmonic humming of his staff and the way the stars seem to align themselves around his head whenever he speaks of the Veil.

Kael seeks to witness the ultimate truth of the universe, yet he is terrified that the truth will be nothing but "Absence"—a void that will consume everything he has spent his life observing.

### Supplemental Lore: The Aspect-Witnessing of Alkyr

In the high peaks of Alkyr, the air is so thin and the Meridian Veil is so close that the laws of physics begin to blur with the laws of perception. "Aspect-Witnessing" is the practice of aligning one's internal resonance with the frequencies of the higher nodes.

Those who achieve this state are called "Witnesses." They no longer see the world through light and shadow, but through "Aspects"—the fundamental concepts and energies that make up reality. A Witness might see a tree not as wood and leaves, but as a "Pulse of Growth" and a "Memory of Earth." However, this state is extremely taxing on the human mind. Most Witnesses eventually lose their ability to perceive the physical world entirely, becoming "Star-Bound" entities that drift through the higher reaches of the Veil until they are consumed by the Absence.

### The Aether-Wood Staff of Kael

Kael’s staff is made from the wood of a tree that grew entirely within a node-field. It is more of an instrument than a tool.

1. **The Resonance-Core:** A hollow chamber in the center of the staff that amplifies the "Music of the Spheres."
2. **The Obsidian Tip:** Used to "anchor" Kael's consciousness to the physical world while he is witnessing.
