Marek Toll — The Crossroads-Collector

Slug: marek_toll.md
Region: Valen

A greedy and opportunistic tax-collector for the Great Crossroads of Valen, Marek is responsible for collecting the "Trade-Glyphs" required for passage through the region's node-paths. He is a man of large appetites and little conscience, his life dedicated to the accumulation of wealth and influence.

Marek is a large, portly man with skin the color of sun-baked clay. He wears an expensive, multi-layered silk robe decorated with intricate trade-glyphs and planetary diagrams. His fingers are adorned with heavy gold rings, and he carries a large, iron-bound strongbox and a series of "Glyph-Stamps"—specialized tools used for recording and verifying trade-contracts. He smells of expensive incense, fine wine, and the sharp, metallic scent of heated copper.

Marek serves as an antagonist and a source of social friction. He provides the Traveler with the "Trade-Sigils" needed to bypass the region's checkpoints, but only in exchange for a significant and dangerous "Resource-Debt."

The rhythmic *clink* of the gold rings on his fingers and the soft, satisfied hum he produces while he is counting his wealth.

Marek seeks to accumulate wealth to protect himself from the world's decay, yet he is haunted by the realization that the very wealth he is accumulating is losing its value as the world’s nodes collapse, making him a collector of worthless treasures.

### Supplemental Lore: The Trade-Glyphs of Valen

In the golden grasslands of Valen, commerce is a form of "Metaphysical Exchange." The "Trade-Glyphs" that govern the region's markets are etched into copper and gold using specialized tools and node-oil. This process "anchors" the value of goods and services into the physical world, ensuring that prices, quality, and even delivery behave according to the dictates of the Guild-Masters.

The Crossroads-Collectors are the enforcers of this commerce. They are trained to perceive the "Friction" caused by unfair trade—a subtle, vibrating heat that emanates from those transactions that are no longer in sync with the Guild’s laws. By applying different types of "Glyph-Stamps" to these transactions, a collector can restore the market's stability. However, the process is extremely taxing on the collector's mind and body. Constant exposure to the raw energy of the trade-glyphs can lead to "Greed-Sickness," a condition where the collector's own skin takes on the texture of polished gold, as they literally become part of the wealth they serve.

### The Strongbox of Marek

Marek’s strongbox is more than a storage container; it is a "Metaphysical Vault."

1. **The Iron-Bound Seal:** Provides the pressure needed to keep the trade-glyphs from leaking their energy.
2. **The Glyph-Reservoir:** A small chamber in the base that stores the "Value-Residue" harvested from traders, used to power Marek's own research into the Fracture.
