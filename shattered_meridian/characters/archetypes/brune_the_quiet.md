A mysterious and silent guardian of the "Silent Shore" in Mirel, Brune is a man who has traded his voice for the ability to perceive the "True-Mist"—the underlying reality beneath the region's deceptive echoes. He is a figure of heavy presence and absolute stillness, his life dedicated to protecting the secrets of the marsh.

Brune is a massive, broad-shouldered man, his body wrapped in a heavy, tattered cloak of grey-green wool that seems to absorb all light and sound. His face is hidden behind a simple, featureless mask of dark wood, and he never speaks, communicating only through simple gestures and the "Mist-Signals" he creates with his hands. He carries a heavy, iron-bound staff and a series of "Silence-Charms"—small, carved stones that dampen all sound in their vicinity. He smells of damp earth, stagnant water, and the sharp, clean scent of rain.

Brune serves as a guardian and guide to the world's most dangerous secrets. He provides the Traveler with the "Silence-Charms" needed to bypass the "Echo-Traps" of Mirel and helps them find the hidden paths to the Hollow.

Brune seeks to protect the world's secrets, yet he is haunted by the realization that the secrets he is guarding are the very things that caused the Fracture, making him a protector of the world's destruction.

The moment the stone touched the Traveler’s hand, the world went silent. The sound of the water, the wind, and even their own breathing vanished, replaced by an absolute, heavy stillness. Brune nodded, then turned and began to walk through the mist, his staff producing a soft, muffled *thud* that only the Traveler could hear. He moved with a slow, deliberate grace, as if he were walking on the surface of a dream. The Traveler followed, realizing that in this place, silence was not just a lack of sound; it was a shield.

### Supplemental Lore: The Silence-Charms of Mirel

In the shrouded coast of Mirel, sound is a dangerous resource. The "Echo-Traps" that saturate the region are sensitive to the vibrations of the physical world, using them to anchor and amplify the deceptive images of the past. The "Silence-Charms" are devices created by the Sentinels to counteract this phenomenon.

These charms are made from "Absence-Stone"—a rare material found only in the deepest parts of the marsh that has the property of absorbing all kinetic and acoustic energy. By carving specific "Silence-Glyphs" into the stone, a Sentinel can create a localized zone of absolute stillness. This allows them to move through the marsh without triggering the traps, but it also isolates them from the world around them. Constant use of the charms can lead to "Sensory-Deprivation Sickness," a condition where the user's mind begins to hallucinate in the absence of external stimuli.

### The Staff of Brune

Brune’s staff is made of "Mire-Oak"—a wood that has been petrified by the region's node-residue.

1. **The Iron-Bound Grip:** Provides the weight needed to break through the "Echo-Walls."
2. **The Silence-Core:** A small chamber in the center of the staff that contains a high-purity Absence-Stone.
