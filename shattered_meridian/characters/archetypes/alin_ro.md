A wise and compassionate elder from the floating groves of Thalen, Alin is a "Root-Singer"—a specialized healer and mediator who uses the "Spirit-Sap" of the region's massive trees to treat the physical and metaphysical wounds caused by the Fracture. He is a man of quiet strength and deep empathy, his life dedicated to the preservation of the region's harmony.

Alin is a tall, slender man with skin the color of aged oak. He wears a simple robe of woven spirit-silk and a series of "Sap-Vials"—small, glass containers filled with iridescent oils—strapped to his belt. His hair is a long, flowing mane of white-silver, and his eyes are a soft, warm amber. He carries a "Root-Staff"—a long, curved branch of spirit-wood etched with healing-glyphs. He smells of damp earth, sweet sap, and the sharp, clean scent of pine.

Alin serves as a healer and a source of spiritual guidance. He provides the Traveler with the "Spirit-Sap" needed to stabilize their own internal resonance and helps them understand the dangerous potential of the Fracture’s mental toll.

### Supplemental Lore: The Root-Singing of Thalen

In the floating groves of Thalen, healing is a form of "Metaphysical Mediation." The "Spirit-Sap" that flows through the region's massive trees is not just a biological fluid; it is "Condensed Resonance" that contains the memories and emotions of the world. The "Root-Singers" are the practitioners of this art.

They are trained to perceive the "Discord" caused by metaphysical wounds—a subtle, vibrating heat that emanates from those parts of the Meridian Lattice that are no longer in sync with the world's harmony. By singing specific "Root-Songs" and applying different types of "Sap-Vials" to these areas, a singer can restore the world's stability. However, the process is extremely taxing on the singer's mind and body. Constant exposure to the raw energy of the sap can lead to "Spirit-Sickness," a condition where the singer's own internal sense of self becomes detached from reality, as they literally become part of the song they serve.

### The Root-Staff of Alin

Alin’s staff is more than a tool; it is a "Metaphysical Siphon."

1. **The Spirit-Wood:** Provides the resonance needed to channel the energy of the spirit-trees.
2. **The Healing-Glyphs:** Etched into the staff, these glyphs allow Alin to "tune" the sap to a specific frequency, allowing it to heal physical and metaphysical wounds.
