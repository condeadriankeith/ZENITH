Toran Ironsund — The Oath-Breaker

Slug: toran_ironsund.md
Region: Eirn

A disgraced warrior of the Frostbound Oathlands, Toran was once a celebrated champion of the Eirn Clans until he broke his "Ice-Breath Oath" to save a village from a collapsing node. Now, he lives as a pariah, his blood literally freezing in his veins.

Toran is a mountain of a man, his skin a pale, frost-bitten blue. He wears heavy furs and rusted chainmail that is perpetually covered in a thin layer of frost. His eyes are a piercing, icy white, and his breath comes out in thick, frozen clouds even in the warmest weather. He carries a massive, notched claymore made of "Glacial-Iron" that he uses more like a walking stick than a weapon. He smells of old snow, rusted metal, and the sharp tang of a coming blizzard.

Toran serves as a tragic mirror to the Traveler—a man who lost his identity and his place in the world to do what was right. He provides the physical muscle and the grim determination needed to survive the world's harshest environments.

The sound of his heavy, ice-crusted boots crunching on stone and the way the air temperature drops significantly whenever he enters a room.

Toran is an "Oath-Breaker" who values honor above all else, yet he is forced to live a life of perceived dishonor because he chose the lives of others over the rigid laws of his clan.

- Act I — Ch. 10 (The Frozen Passes): Toran is found living in a cave, guarding a mountain pass. He initially fights the Traveler, but joins them after seeing the "Hollow-Sign" on the Traveler's compass.
- Act II — Ch. 22 (The Glacial Vault): Toran uses his "Frozen Blood" to stabilize a freezing node-core, nearly dying in the process to allow the Traveler to retrieve a vital artifact.
- Act III — Ch. 30 (The Final Stand): Toran holds the line against a wave of "Absence-Wraiths" at the edge of the Hollow, his body finally shattering into ice as he fulfills his "Final Oath" to the Traveler.

### Supplemental Lore: The Oaths of Eirn

In the Frostbound Oathlands, survival is a metaphysical achievement. The Eirn Clans believe that the human body is naturally "leaky" and that without the structure of an oath, a person's warmth and identity will simply bleed out into the frozen wastes.

An "Ice-Breath Oath" is a ritual where a warrior breathes their intent into a shard of Glacial-Iron. If the oath is kept, the iron stays warm, providing a source of heat for the warrior. If the oath is broken, the iron turns to "Absolute Ice," a substance that absorbs heat from everything it touches, including the warrior's own blood. This is the "Frost-Bleed," a slow, agonizing process where the victim's body gradually turns into a living glacier.

### The Claymore of Toran

Toran’s sword is no longer a weapon of war; it is a "Heat-Sink." Because it is made of the iron he used for his broken oath, it is constantly drawing heat from the environment.

1. **The Frost-Guard:** The hilt is permanently frozen to Toran's hand, meaning he can never truly put the sword down.
2. **The Shatter-Edge:** The blade is so cold that it doesn't cut; it causes the structure of whatever it touches to become brittle and shatter.
