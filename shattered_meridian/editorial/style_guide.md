# Style Guide

## Prose Style

### Tone and Voice
- **Cinematic:** Write scenes that readers can visualize as film sequences
- **Sensory:** Emphasize tactile, visual, auditory, and other sensory details
- **Lean:** Avoid overly elaborate or flowery prose (purple prose)
- **Consistent:** Maintain voice throughout the work

### Language and Diction
- Use evocative but clear language
- Prefer active voice over passive voice
- Choose precise, specific words over vague ones
- Maintain formal register appropriate to epic fantasy

### Sentence Structure
- Vary sentence length for rhythm and emphasis
- Use strong, active verbs
- Eliminate unnecessary modifiers
- Ensure clarity while maintaining atmosphere

## Narrative Techniques

### Show vs. Tell
- Demonstrate character emotions through action and dialogue
- Reveal world elements through interaction and experience
- Show consequences rather than explaining them
- Use specific details instead of generalizations

### Pacing
- Balance action with reflection
- Vary the rhythm of scenes
- Use shorter sentences for tension
- Use longer, flowing sentences for description

### Perspective
- Maintain clear point of view
- Stay within the character's knowledge and experience
- Use internal monologue sparingly and purposefully
- Ground the reader in the character's perspective

## World Building Integration

### Magic System Presentation
- Show magic through its effects and costs
- Describe the visual, auditory, and tactile aspects
- Demonstrate the mechanics through action
- Emphasize the consequences and limitations

### Cultural Details
- Integrate cultural elements naturally into scenes
- Show customs through character behavior
- Use dialogue to reveal cultural norms
- Avoid exposition dumps about culture

### Environmental Description
- Ground readers in the setting through sensory details
- Use setting to reflect mood and theme
- Make environment interactive rather than decorative
- Connect setting to character experience

## Character Development

### Voice and Dialogue
- Give each character a distinct speaking pattern
- Ensure dialogue serves plot, character, or thematic purposes
- Use subtext to add depth to conversations
- Match speech patterns to character background and personality

### Internal Experience
- Show character thoughts through action and reaction
- Use physical responses to convey emotion
- Demonstrate character growth through changed responses
- Balance internal reflection with external action

### Consistency
- Maintain character traits and constraints consistently
- Keep character knowledge and abilities realistic
- Ensure character motivations are clear and believable
- Track character development arcs across scenes

## Thematic Elements

### Motif Usage
- **Prismatic Imagery:** Use oil-on-water, shifting hues consistently for magical moments
- **Left-Hand Motif:** Maintain the left-hand focus for bindings and inversions
- **Sensory Details:** Use metallic tastes, reedy tones, prismatic lights consistently

### Theme Integration
- Weave themes through action, dialogue, and description
- Avoid heavy-handed thematic statements
- Use character choices to explore themes
- Let themes emerge naturally from the narrative

## Technical Elements

### Paragraph Length
- Keep exposition paragraphs under 400 words
- Vary paragraph lengths for rhythm
- Use shorter paragraphs for action
- Use longer paragraphs for description when appropriate

### Scene Structure
- Begin scenes with clear setting and character placement
- End scenes with forward momentum or clear resolution
- Ensure smooth transitions between scenes
- Maintain narrative flow within scenes

### Canonical Requirements
- Preserve canonical phrases verbatim where required
- Maintain character constraints consistently
- Follow magic system rules without contradiction
- Respect established world-building elements

## Revision Standards

### First Draft Focus
- Complete the narrative arc
- Establish character voices
- Integrate world-building elements
- Set up thematic elements

### Revision Focus
- Strengthen prose style
- Enhance sensory details
- Improve pacing and rhythm
- Refine thematic integration

### Final Polish
- Check for canonical compliance
- Verify character consistency
- Ensure world-building coherence
- Polish prose for clarity and impact
