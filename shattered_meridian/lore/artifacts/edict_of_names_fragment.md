## Archivist Note
This fragment is one of the few surviving direct accounts of the "First Ordering." The language is archaic, but the intent is clear: the Architects viewed the world as a delicate instrument of sound and light. The "Meddling" mentioned in the refrain is not just a warning against pride, but a warning against "un-tuning" the world's fundamental frequency.
