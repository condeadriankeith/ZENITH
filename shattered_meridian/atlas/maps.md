## Map Legend

- **Nodes (N-XX):** Points of high Meridian concentration. These are the "servers" of the world.
- **Leylines:** The invisible flows of energy connecting the nodes.
- **The Hollow:** The ultimate quarantine zone; no leylines lead out, only in.
- **Fracture Lines:** Areas where the Meridian Veil has thinned, usually near Nyth or Sahru.
- **The Meridian Veil:** The metaphysical strata that overlies all coordinates.
