# Traveler's Route — *The Shattered Meridian*

## Phase 1: The Awakening (The Hollow)
- **Starting Point:** The Hollow (N-11).
- **Objective:** Retrieve the first fragment of the Archivist's promise.
- **Node Interaction:** Bypassing the initial Hollow Lock using a left-hand rite.
- **Narrative Tie:** Prologue — The touch of the Book.

## Phase 2: The Law's Shadow (Cyris to Valen)
- **Path:** Traveling north through the marble terraces of Cyris (N-02) to the Central Plain of Valen (N-01).
- **Node Interaction:** Witnessing the Trial in Cyris; observing the Pillar of Oaths.
- **Narrative Tie:** Chapter 3 — The cost of a mother's healing.

## Phase 3: The Engine's Failure (Valen to Virek)
- **Path:** Eastward into the brass towers of Virek (N-03).
- **Node Interaction:** The Foundry Rupture; first encounter with the active Meridian Quarantine.
- **Narrative Tie:** Chapter 7 — The Mercury-Keel legacy.

## Phase 4: The Spirit's Echo (Virek to Thalen)
- **Path:** Southward into the living groves of Thalen (N-04).
- **Node Interaction:** Naming a tree; trading a memory for a spirit's cooperation.
- **Narrative Tie:** Chapter 12 — The Green-Gold Prismatic.

## Phase 5: The Deep Ledger (Thalen to Kallum)
- **Path:** Westward to the reedy docks of Kallum (N-05).
- **Node Interaction:** Consulting the Salt-Verse; recovering the name of the plague child.
- **Narrative Tie:** Chapter 15 — The weight of the bone-ledger.

## Phase 6: The Final Ascent (Kallum to Alkyr)
- **Path:** A long trek north to the sheer cliffs of Alkyr (N-07).
- **Node Interaction:** The Star-Shard attunement; witnessing the Veil directly.
- **Narrative Tie:** Chapter 20 — The Starward Climb.

## Phase 7: The Return to the Center (Alkyr to The Hollow)
- **Path:** A final descent back to the site of the Fracture.
- **Node Interaction:** Re-tuning the triune helix; the choice of Restoration, Replacement, or Confirmation.
- **Narrative Tie:** Finale — The Shattered Meridian.
