# Bestiary

## Spirit Types & Notable Creatures

### Place-Spirits
- **Description:** Emergent entities bound to specific locations; relatively stable unless their node is damaged.
- **Behavior:** Generally neutral unless their territory is threatened or their node experiences entropy buildup.
- **Interaction:** May offer aid to those who respect their domain; hostile to those who exploit or damage their space.
- **Weakness:** Vulnerable to node disruption or changes in the physical landscape.

### Memory-Echoes
- **Description:** Ephemeral spirits that carry single recollections or songs; often found near sites of significant emotional events.
- **Behavior:** Passive, replaying their stored memory repeatedly; may follow individuals who resonate with their stored emotion.
- **Interaction:** Harmless but can be unsettling when they repeat old conversations or sounds.
- **Weakness:** Fade over time or when the emotional resonance of their location diminishes.

### Oath-Wights
- **Description:** Formed from broken promises; aggressive to meaning and truth.
- **Behavior:** Seek to corrupt or break oaths, contracts, and promises; feed on the dissolution of social bonds.
- **Interaction:** Dangerous to those who rely on verbal agreements; can make spoken words unreliable.
- **Weakness:** Strengthened by fulfilled promises and reinforced contracts.

### Hunger-Spirits
- **Description:** Arise near high-entropy nodes; voracious for names and identities.
- **Behavior:** Drift toward populated areas seeking to consume personal and proper names.
- **Interaction:** Cause memory gaps and identity confusion; responsible for early stages of Name-Bleed.
- **Weakness:** Repelled by strong, well-maintained names and communities with robust naming traditions.

### Revenants (Sahru-type)
- **Description:** Former humans turned memory-hungry, focusing on collection of names; result of failed ascension attempts.
- **Behavior:** Systematically hunt for names and identities; organize others into nameless hordes.
- **Interaction:** Extremely dangerous; transform environments into Name-Bleed zones; King Haraes is the most famous example.
- **Weakness:** Bound to the location of their transformation; powerful naming rituals can banish them.

### Fissure-Drake
- **Description:** Large aberration that forms near reality slippage; feasts on conceptual cohesion.
- **Behavior:** Creates zones where concepts become unstable; causes logical contradictions and paradoxes.
- **Interaction:** Devastating to settlements; makes abstract thought difficult; reality becomes malleable in their presence.
- **Weakness:** Requires high-entropy environments to sustain themselves; can be driven away with stabilized node energy.

### Ley-Stag
- **Description:** Migratory majestic deer whose antlers condense Meridian energy; considered omens.
- **Behavior:** Travel in herds along leyline paths; migrate seasonally following energy flows.
- **Interaction:** Generally benevolent; their presence indicates healthy node networks; sighting is considered lucky.
- **Weakness:** Sensitive to node disruption; flee areas experiencing entropy buildup.

## Regional Variants

### Cyris - Marble Dominion
- **Oath-Guards:** Semi-spiritual guardians that enforce contractual obligations; appear as marble figures.
- **Ledger-Wraiths:** Spirits that haunt incomplete or fraudulent legal documents; ensure accuracy in records.

### Thalen - Verdant Confluence
- **Grove-Speakers:** Conscious trees that remember the names of all who passed beneath them; repositories of local history.
- **Song-Birds:** Avians that carry and preserve melodies; their songs can restore damaged memories.

### Virek - Brass Ascendancy
- **Rune-Foxes:** Small creatures made of living brass script; repair damaged mechanisms and corrupted formulas.
- **Steam-Sprites:** Elemental beings of engineered magic; assist with precision crafting and calculations.

### Kallum - Saltbound Expanse
- **Tide-Whales:** Massive spirits that swim through memory-liquids; preserve the names of sailors lost at sea.
- **Bone-Crabs:** Small spirits that collect and preserve fragments of names; help maintain ledger integrity.

### Sahru - Sunken Wastes
- **Silence-Moths:** Beings that feed on spoken words; cause areas of enforced quietude.
- **Ash-Hounds:** Spirits of failed ascension attempts; hunt those who attempt unauthorized rituals.

### Alkyr - Starward Climb
- **Aspect-Echoes:** Fragments of Architect consciousness; provide glimpses of cosmic perspective.
- **Star-Chasers:** Beings that move between the mortal realm and the Veil; facilitate communion with higher powers.

### Mirel - Shrouded Coast
- **Mist-Walkers:** Entities that exist partially in memory-space; can move between different temporal layers.
- **Echo-Lynx:** Predators that hunt false memories; preserve authenticity in recollection.

### Nyth - Fracture Beyond
- **Concept-Cats:** Beings that embody abstract ideas; help maintain logical consistency.
- **Void-Spiders:** Weavers of conceptual boundaries; prevent ontological collapse in anti-ontology zones.

### Valen - Central Plain
- **Crossroad-Foxes:** Intelligent spirits that remember all paths taken and not taken; guide travelers.
- **Trade-Sprites:** Small beings that ensure fair exchange; punish those who cheat in bargains.

## Encountering Creatures

Most supernatural entities in the Shattered Meridian world are not inherently evil but operate according to different value systems than humans. Understanding their motivations and needs is crucial for safe interaction:

- **Respect territorial boundaries** and the integrity of local nodes
- **Maintain proper naming** and identity practices
- **Honor commitments** and avoid breaking oaths
- **Approach with humility** rather than attempting to dominate

## Countermeasures

- **Salt-circle protection** (Kallum tradition): Preserves names and identities
- **Marble oaths** (Cyris tradition): Creates barriers against oath-breaking entities
- **Living-name rituals** (Thalen tradition): Maintains vitality of personal and place names
- **Rune-warding** (Virek tradition): Technological protections against entity intrusion
- **Star-warding** (Alkyr tradition): Astral protections against conceptual attacks
