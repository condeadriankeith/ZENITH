# Lore Update Summary: Shattered Meridian Overhaul

## 1. Executive Summary
This update implements a comprehensive "Cosmic Lore" layer to the *Shattered Meridian* worldbuilding. It transitions the project from a standard fantasy setting to a high-concept metaphysical world governed by the **Meridian Lattice**, the **Spirit Mirror**, and the **Quarantine**.

## 2. Key Changes
### A. Metaphysics & Systems
- **The Meridian Lattice:** Established the world's physical skeleton as a network of Nodes and Cores.
- **Spirit Mirror & Name-Bleed:** Defined the sensory consequences of metaphysical instability. "Names" are now a tangible resource/currency.
- **Spirit Resonance (SR):** Introduced a technical measure for mortal alignment with the Spirit Mirror.
- **Quarantine Response:** Defined the automated defense system (Toll, Net, Pruning).

### B. World Lore
- **Edict of Names:** Formalized the refrain "Mortals should not meddle with the power of the Gods" as a fundamental world-law.
- **The Architects:** Clarified their role as system managers (Celestials) who left behind "Residual Authority."
- **The Fracture (907 R):** Solidified the canonical origin event involving the Arc-Foundry of Virek.
