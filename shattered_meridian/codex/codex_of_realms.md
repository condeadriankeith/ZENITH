## The Mortal Face & The Meridian Veil

**Mortal Face**: The observable world — land, sea, cities, living beings. Politics and desires play out here.

**Meridian Veil**: An interleaved metaphysical strata of memory, pattern, and procedural law. It records and regulates recurring processes: seasons, oaths, rites, mechanisms of ascension. Ley-veins are concentrated flows of compressed Meridian; nodes are local concentrations (like servers in a network).

**Key idea:** The Veil is not spiritual fluff — it is procedural: it enforces and records. Tampering with it has technical consequences.

## The Architects (Aspects)

Architects are meta-agents — patterns that maintained cycles: growth, law, invention, memory, and endings. They were sometimes visible in culture as "gods," but they are better thought of as *systemic managers* rather than moral beings.

**Common Aspects (conceptual; not anthropomorphic):**
- **Cyclewright** — seasons, growth, harvest accounting.
- **Binder** — law, oaths, ritual contracts.
- **Forger** — invention, transformation, ordered change.
- **Deep-Mother** — sea memory, mourning, names.
- **Shroud** — endings, concealment, sleep.

## The Meridian Quarantine

**Definition:** A semi-sentient, distributed defense network composed of activated Meridian Locks and residual Architect-logic. It responds to breaches in the Meridian's integrity with corrective penalties: node sealing, memory famine, revenant conversion, or local reality slippage.

**Goals:** Preserve the structural coherence of the Meridian Veil at any cost. It acts without malice but with rigid purpose. Because it enforces preservation, it actively opposes mortal meddling that threatens long-term stability.

**Narrative function:** The Quarantine gives a visible, reactive force to oppose or test the Traveler's actions.

## The Vanishing & The Fracture

**Canonical origin (chosen truth):** The *Fracture* — an event in Year 907 R — occurred when an Arc-Foundry (Virek) test using a Mercury-Keel prototype attempted to temporal-shift a node in the Treaty of Three Stones crossing. The node's feedback resonated with Architect-patterns. The Meridian Quarantine activated network-wide, sealing architects' direct signatures from the Mortal Face. Architects were either cut off, sealed, or retreated to the Veil. This coordinated activation is what cultures call the Vanishing.

> In the year 907 of the Reckoning, the Treaty of Three Stones convened its engineers, ritualists, and archivists upon a crossing of leylines in the Central Plain. The Arc-Foundry's Mercury-Keel prototype, designed to "phase" a node for temporal observations, was calibrated to a new range. That night the node sang not a local song but an old pattern—an Architect-signature. The Quarantine awoke in a way that had not happened for an age. Locks spread like a cold net, sealing nodes, cutting flows, and severing Architects from their Mortal work. The world felt like a house whose doors had been slammed suddenly. What men called the Vanishing is the echo of that slam. Cities changed, treaties collapsed, and the Meridian became a guarded thing. The Hollow's Seal was keyed that night.
>
> — *Codex Courts, Fracture Dossier, annotated*

## The Three Laws of Ascension

> **THE THREE LAWS OF ASCENSION**
> 1. **Irrecoverable Cost** — An ascension consumes a fragment of the aspirant; it is lost forever.
> 2. **Node Entropy** — Ascension leaves a permanent mark upon the node; repeated use risks quarantine.
> 3. **Political Targeting** — Unsanctioned Ascension invites enforcement; it cannot be completed in secret.

These laws govern all ascension attempts and serve as fundamental constraints in the world. They are not mere warnings but immutable rules with consequences that manifest regardless of intent.

## Cultural Detail: The Village Naming Ritual

In the rural valleys of the Central Plain, where the influence of Cyris’s legalism is felt but not fully enforced, naming is a communal act. A child is born under a "Temporary Bond," recorded in a cradle-ledger of polished bone. This temporary name remains fluid, a placeholder until the child’s first resonance with the Meridian is observed. If the local well hums a reedy tone—an omen of Name-Bleed—the name is often considered "thin," a debt already partially claimed by the Quarantine. These communities believe that a name is not a gift, but a loan from the Meridian, one that must be stabilized through ritual or, in dire cases, traded to preserve the village’s collective memory.

## Chronology of Ages

- **Age Before Reckoning (mythic):** Pre-historic formation myths and origin tales.
- **Age of Weft (0–400 R):** Architects openly interact; first codices; initial sanctioned ascensions.
- **Age of Edifice (400–900 R):** Cities institutionalize arcana; guilds.
- **The Fracture (907 R):** Arc-Foundry experiment triggers the Meridian Quarantine activation.
- **Age of Tension (908–1299 R):** Wars, first false ascensions (Sahru 972 R), node corruption.
- **The Great Sealing (1300–1315 R):** coordinated activation of large-scale Meridian Locks and systematic sealing of high-order access.
- **Age of Long Night (1315–1600 R):** Fragmentation of societies.
- **Era of Rediscovery (1601–present):** Return of the Traveler-era seekers, experiments, and the Hollow reawakening.
