# The Physics of Shards
**Rule 1:** Shards are radioactive. They emit "Resonance."
**Rule 2:** Proximity causes "The Bleed."
    * **Mind Shard Exposure:** You lose names.
    * **Life Shard Exposure:** You grow tumors/moss.
    * **Body Shard Exposure:** Your bones shift/liquefy.
**Rule 3:** Shards *want* to be together. They vibrate harder when close to other Shards.
