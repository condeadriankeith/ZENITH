# Region 3: The Deep Weald (Sahru)
**Dominant Shard:** Veridian (Life).
**Archetype:** Overgrowth.
**Atmosphere:** Humid, Bioluminescent, Poisonous.
**The Threat:** The forest grows faster than it can be cut. Trees have "heartbeats."
**Key Location:** The Root-Heart (Where the Beast of Root sleeps).
