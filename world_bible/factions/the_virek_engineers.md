# The Virek Engineers (Technology)
**Leader:** Chief Engineer Coren Astell.
**Units:**
* **God-Engines:** Massive mechanical constructs powered by Shards.
* **Tech-Priests:** Engineers who worship technology as divine.
**Philosophy:** "Progress Through Power."

## Motivation
**Goal:** EXTRACTION
**Motivation:** To find and use the Shards as power sources for their "God-Engines." They see the Shards as the ultimate fuel for industrial and technological advancement.
