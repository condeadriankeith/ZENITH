# The Magistrates (Law)
**Leader:** High Inquisitor Oren Valis.
**Units:**
* **White Skiffs:** Silent hover-drones.
* **Inquisitors:** Agents with Mercury-Canes.
**Philosophy:** "Silence is Safety."

## Motivation
**Goal:** CONTAINMENT
**Motivation:** To hide the Shards and enforce the Law of Silence. They believe that by keeping the Shards dormant and hidden, they can prevent the Great Filter from triggering.
