# The White Hand (Body)
**Leader:** High Priest Kaelen.
**Units:**
* **The Ascended:** Soldiers who have replaced skin with bone-plate.
* **Flesh-Golems:** stitched-together mounds of muscle.
**Philosophy:** "Pain is Evolution."

## Motivation
**Goal:** EVOLUTION
**Motivation:** To become the Shards through "Ascension Rites." They believe that humanity must mutate and harden to survive the coming Celestial Reset, effectively becoming cosmic entities themselves.
