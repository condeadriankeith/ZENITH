# The Age of Inheritance (The Golden Age)
**Timeline:** 1,000 Years Ago.
**The State of the World:** Humanity lived in a single, boundless city called "Aethelgard."
**The Magic:** There were no Shards. Magic (Resonance) was atmospheric and free.
**The Event:** The "Great Over-Reach." Scientists tried to tap into the "Star-Altar" (The Zenith) to achieve immortality.
**The Result:** They drew the attention of Val-Kora. The Celestial arrived to "prune" the reality.
