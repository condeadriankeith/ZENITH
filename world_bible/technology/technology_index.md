# Technology Index

## Old Tech vs New Tech

- **Old Tech:** Pneumatic, steam, brass (Virek).
- **New Tech:** Hover-drones, silent propulsion, clean energy (Cyris).

## Engines and Skiffs

The world features two distinct technological paradigms:

### Old Tech (Virek Region)
- Steam-powered machinery
- Brass construction materials
- Pneumatic systems
- Industrial-scale manufacturing

### New Tech (Cyris Region)
- Clean energy sources
- Silent propulsion systems
- Hover-drone technology
- Advanced materials science
