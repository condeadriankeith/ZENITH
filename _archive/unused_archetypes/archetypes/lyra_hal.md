A brilliant but reclusive astronomer from the Starward Climb, Lyra uses a massive, mountain-top telescope to map the "Celestial Nodes"—the higher-order structures of the Meridian Veil. She is a woman of quiet intensity and vast knowledge, her mind always fixed on the stars.

Lyra is tall and slender, her skin a pale, moonlight silver. She wears long, flowing robes of midnight-blue silk, decorated with intricate star-charts and planetary diagrams. Her eyes are a deep, shimmering violet, and she carries a small, brass "Star-Globe" that she uses to calculate the movements of the Veil. She smells of old parchment, cold mountain air, and the faint, ozone-like scent of aether.

Lyra serves as a source of cosmic information and a guide to the world's higher structures. She provides the Traveler with the "Star-Charts" needed to navigate the celestial node-paths and helps them understand the true scale of the Fracture.

## Relationship to World Shards
- **Motivation:** Lyra maps the "Name-Bleed" as if it were a new galaxy. She believes the Shards are rewriting the map of Aethelgard.

- She maps the future but has no place in it.

### Supplemental Lore: The Star-Mapping of Alkyr

In the high peaks of Alkyr, the atmosphere is so thin that the Meridian Veil becomes visible to the naked eye. This has led to the development of "Star-Mapping"—the art of tracking the movements and health of the "Celestial Nodes." These nodes are not physical objects, but concentrated points of energy in the higher reaches of the Veil that govern the world's larger patterns—weather, seasons, and the flow of time.

The Star-Mappers of Alkyr use massive, mountain-top telescopes to observe these nodes. These telescopes are not just optical instruments; they are "Resonant Collectors" that can detect the subtle vibrations and frequency shifts of the nodes. By mapping these shifts, a skilled astronomer can predict the onset of node-storms and the location of "Safe-Paths" through the Veil. However, the constant exposure to the raw energy of the sky takes a toll on the astronomer's mind. Many Star-Mappers eventually become "Veil-Lost," their consciousnesses drifting away from the physical world to live entirely within the patterns of the stars.

### The Star-Globe of Lyra

Lyra’s star-globe is a "Metaphysical Calculator."

1. **The Aether-Core:** A small, glowing sphere of compressed node-residue that powers the globe's calculations.
2. **The Brass-Rings:** Each ring represents a different level of the Meridian Veil, and can be adjusted to simulate the effects of a node-shift.
