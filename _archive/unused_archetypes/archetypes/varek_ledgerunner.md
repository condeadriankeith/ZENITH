Varek the Fallout-Tracker — The Enforcer of the Oily Deep

Region: Kallum

A ruthless and efficient enforcer for the Kallum Tide-Merchants, Varek is responsible for tracking the "Fallout-Exposure" of those who scavenge the region's Shard-residue. He is a man of cold calculation and unwavering focus, his life governed by the rigid monitoring of Arcane Radiation.

Varek is a tall, thin man with skin that looks like pale, water-logged parchment. He wears a heavy, lead-lined cloak and a series of iron-bound radiation-meters strapped to his chest and thighs. His eyes are a sharp, ink-black, and he carries a "Resonance-Hook"—a long, curved blade etched with dampening-glyphs. He smells of salt, ozone, and the metallic tang of Name-Bleed.

Varek serves as an antagonist and a source of systemic pressure. He provides the Traveler with the "Shield-Keys" needed to access the restricted fallout-zones of Kallum, but only in exchange for a significant and dangerous "Fallout-Pledge"—a promise to subject oneself to extreme Name-Bleed for the Merchants' research.

The rhythmic *clink* of the radiation-meters against his legs and the scratching sound of his stylus as he records a new exposure level.

## Relationship to World Shards
- **Motivation:** Varek treats the Shards as "Cosmic Radiation." He believes the Celestials left the world to rot and the Merchants are the only ones keeping the "Filter" at bay through controlled exposure.
- **Goal:** Appraise the potency of the Shards and find a way to "shield" Aethelgard's future—for a price.

## Moral Contradictions
- He is a tracker of Name-Bleed who is already losing his own identity to the very fallout he monitors.
- He claims to be a man of business, but he is secretly obsessed with the "harmonic beauty" of a world being erased.

### Supplemental Lore: The Shard-Hunger of Kallum

In the jagged coasts of Kallum, the Shard-radiation is so thick that the local nodes can only be accessed through "Controlled Name-Bleed." The Tide-Merchants, the region's ruling elite, have institutionalized this process through the creation of "Fallout-Pledges."

When a scavenger uses a node-path or accesses a restricted area, they are required to pledge a portion of their cognitive stability to the Merchants. This "stability" is harvested using specialized resonance-bones—the bleached ribs of deep-sea leviathans that have been treated with Shard-oil. These bones act as capacitors for the user's memories, storing the "human code" before it can be overwritten by the Shard's radiation. These bones are then traded as a form of "Identity-Insurance," providing the wealthy with a backup of their own names while the poor are left to wither into Hollows. The "Fallout-Trackers" are the enforcers of this system, responsible for monitoring exposure levels and ensuring that no one "leaks" too much resonance into the public zones.

### The Resonance-Hook of Varek

Varek’s weapon is more than a blade; it is a "Radiation-Siphon."

1. **The Dampening-Glyphs:** Etched into the steel, these glyphs allow Varek to "catch" a person's resonance and ground it, temporarily stopping their Name-Bleed but also freezing their cognitive functions.
2. **The Lead-Reservoir:** A small chamber in the hilt that stores the "Residue-Ink" harvested from the air around high-resonance subjects, used to record the transaction in the Merchants' logs.
