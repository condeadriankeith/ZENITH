Fenn Smallwatch — The Border-Scout

Slug: fenn_smallwatch.md
Region: Valen

A sharp-eyed and nimble scout for the Border-Guard of Valen, Fenn is responsible for monitoring the "Node-Jitter" along the region's borders. She is a woman of quick reflexes and constant vigilance, her life spent in the golden grasslands and shifting silt-roads of the plains.

Fenn is short and wiry, her skin tanned a deep bronze by the sun of the plains. She wears a light, multi-pocketed scout’s uniform and a series of "Small-Watches"—specialized mechanical devices used for tracking local node-activity—strapped to her arms and belt. Her hair is a short, practical crop of dark brown, and her eyes are a sharp, piercing green. She carries a light, iron-bound staff and a bag of "Signal-Flares." She smells of dry grass, sun-warmed leather, and the sharp, metallic scent of ozone.

Fenn serves as a scout and source of local information. She provides the Traveler with the "Small-Watches" needed to monitor the local node-activity and helps them navigate the deceptive and dangerous terrain of the borders.

The constant, rhythmic *tick-tock* of the watches on her arms and the bright, bird-like whistle she uses to signal her presence.

### Supplemental Lore: The Small-Watches of Valen

In the central plains of Valen, the Meridian Lattice is particularly sensitive to the passage of time and the movements of the nodes. The "Small-Watches" are devices created by the Border-Guard to monitor and influence the "Chronological Pulse" of the local environment.

These watches are not mechanical in the traditional sense; they are "Resonant Oscillators" that vibrate in sympathy with the world's energetic heartbeat. By adjusting the tension and frequency of these watches, a skilled scout can "perceive" the hidden patterns of the nodes and predict the onset of node-storms. However, the process is extremely taxing on the user's mind and body. Constant exposure to the high-intensity vibrations can lead to "Chronological Vertigo," a condition where the user's own internal sense of time becomes detached from reality, leading to hallucinations, memory loss, and a frantic, obsessive need to monitor every passing second.

### The Signal-Flares of Fenn

Fenn’s flares are more than light sources; they are "Resonant Amplifiers."

1. **The Aether-Core:** A small, glowing sphere of compressed node-residue that powers the flare's light.
2. **The Acoustic-Whistle:** Used to "tune" the flare to a specific frequency, allowing it to be heard—and felt—by the nodes.
